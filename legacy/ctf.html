Capture the Flag

Rules

Basics

  • Get the enemy flag, bring it to your side of the boundaries.
  • You get tagged, sit down (clothes included).
  • Your teammates can free you by tagging you.

    • Flags

    • Flags cannot be thrown forwards but CAN be thrown backwards.
    • Flags must be placed in your own territory, in the middle, in a visible place.
    • When you get tagged holding a flag, you drop the flag. It must be placed 15 feet away (laterally).

    Prisoners

    • You must sit down where you were tagged.
    • Out of Bounds: If you go out of bounds in enemy territory, you must sit down where you went out (but inside the boundaries)

    Freeing Prisoners

  • You can free a prisoner by tagging them.
  • When you free a prisoner, you both get a free walk back.
  • Liberators: If you continue forward, you can be tagged out.
  • Freed prisoners: You must walk back to your home side before doing anything else.

    • Guards

    • Guards must not be within 15 feet of prisoners or flags.
